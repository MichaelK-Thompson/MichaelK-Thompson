Data Analyst
------------

Greetings! I'm Michael Thompson, a dedicated and skilled Data Analyst based in the vibrant city of London. My journey in data analysis is backed by a robust foundation in the health sector, enabling me to bring a unique perspective to the field. With a strong passion for extracting patterns and insights from data, I have honed my expertise in a suite of advanced analytical tools and languages. 
My proficiency includes, but is not limited to, **Python**, **SQL**, **R**, **Excel**, **Power BI**, and **Tableau**. These skills empower me to tackle complex datasets, uncover patterns, and present data in a visually compelling manner to inform strategic decisions. Feel free to connect with me for any collaborative opportunities, insights, or discussions related to data analysis in any field! Let's harness the power of data to make a difference!
